At GOMYCODE, we're transforming the educational experience for talents in Africa and the Middle East. We offer an innovative learning approach, diverging from conventional methods. By providing practical and technical skills, we prepare them to excel in the contemporary digital landscape. ­

Mission and Values

­­

At GOMYCODE, our mission is to empower individuals to thrive in the digital era by providing top-notch, hands-on education in technology. We believe in: Interactive Learning and Community Involvement: Foster interactive learning experiences that engage and involve students. "Tell me and I forget, teach me and I may remember, involve me and I learn." - Benjamin Franklin Continuous Learning and Resource Empowerment: Provide a dynamic learning environment that evolves with students. "You can't teach people everything they need to know. The best you can do is position them where they can find what they need to know when they need to know it." - Seymour Papert Mind Training and Critical Thinking: Shift the focus from mere accumulation of facts to the training of minds to think critically. "Education is not the learning of facts, but the training of the mind to think." - Albert Einstein ­­
